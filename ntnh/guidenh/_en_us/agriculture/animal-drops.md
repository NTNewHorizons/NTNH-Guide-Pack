# Animal Drops & Products

> [!NOTE]
> Wild animals only drop 2/3 of the normal amount. Farm animals drop full amounts.

## New Drops

| Mob | Drop | Notes |
|-----|------|-------|
| Bat | Leather | Vanilla: none |
| Wolf | Leather | Vanilla: none |
| Pig | Leather | Configurable chance (30%) |
| Horse | Raw Horse Meat | Configurable amount |
| Squid | Raw Squid Meat | Configurable amount |

## Improved Drops

| Mob | Change |
|-----|--------|
| Zombie Pigman | Cooked Porkchops instead of Rotten Flesh |
| Witches | More potion types, more frequent drops |
| Enderman | Always drops at least 1 Ender Pearl |
| Iron Golem | 1–2 Iron Blocks (in addition to normal drops) |
| Creeper | Configurable TNT drop chance (disabled) |
| Spider | Configurable Cobweb drop chance (6%) |

## Mob Heads & Skulls

Mobs have a configurable chance (10%) to drop their skull on death. Players also have a chance (100%) to drop their head.

## Arrow Retrieval

Some of the arrows used to kill a mob are retrieved (25% chance). This applies to skeletons and any mob that was shot.

## Fireball Cooking

Animals killed by fireballs or explosions drop cooked meat instead of raw.

## Zombie Pigman Curing

Zombie Pigmen can be cured (like Zombie Villagers) using a splash potion of weakness and a golden apple. Cured Pigmen are passive traders that:
- Are passive but fight back if attacked
- Can be traded with
- Occasionally buy armor from you
- Drop porkchops on death
