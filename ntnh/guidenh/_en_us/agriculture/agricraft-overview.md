# AgriCraft Overview

AgriCraft overhauls vanilla farming. Vanilla planting is disabled - you must use **Crop Sticks** on farmland to grow anything. This applies to ALL seeds including sugarcane, cactus, nether wart, and pumpkins.

## Getting Started

Right-click Crop Sticks on tilled soil, then right-click with any seed to plant it. Right-click again with more Crop Sticks to turn it into a **Crosscrop** (used for breeding).

| Item | Usage |
|------|-------|
| Crop Sticks | Plant crops; craft with 4 sticks |
| Seed Analyzer | Reveals a seed's stats (Growth, Gain, Strength) |
| Magnifying Glass | Inspect planted crops without harvesting |
| Trowel | Pick up a planted crop without destroying it |
| Hand Rake | Remove weeds safely |
| Clipper | Harvest seed clones from mature crops |

## Tools

**Seed Analyzer** - Place a seed inside to see its Growth, Gain, and Strength ratings (1-10). Using it also fills your **Journal** with crop data.

**Magnifying Glass** - Right-click any planted crop to see its current stats and species without breaking it.

**Trowel** - Shift-right-click a crop to pick it up and place it elsewhere. The crop keeps its stats.

**Hand Rake** - Right-click weeds to remove them. May drop useful items.

**Clipper** - Right-click a mature crop to get a seed copy. The crop stays planted.

> [!TIP]
> Always analyze new seeds in the Seed Analyzer before planting so they appear in your Journal.

## Bonemeal

Bonemeal works on AgriCraft crops to speed growth, but **does not force mutations** on Crosscrops. Right-click a planted crop to accelerate it.

## Irrigation System

AgriCraft adds a water-based irrigation system to speed crop growth:

| Component | Function |
|-----------|----------|
| Wooden Water Tank | Stores water (fills from rain or nearby water) |
| Wooden Irrigation Channel | Transports water between tanks and sprinklers |
| Sprinkler | Waters nearby crops, causing growth ticks |

A sprinkler gives each plant in range a 20% chance to growth tick every 5 seconds, using 10mb of water per second from the network.

## Seed Storage

The **Seed Storage** system lets you bulk-store analyzed seeds. Place a Seed Storage Controller next to chests to auto-organize seeds by type and stats.

## Weeds

Weeds can randomly spawn on empty Crop Sticks or Crosscrops. They spread quickly if left unchecked. Use a **Hand Rake** to remove them. Enable Magnifying Glass inspection to spot weeds early.
