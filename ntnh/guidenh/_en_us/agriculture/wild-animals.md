# Wild Animals & Domestication

Animals found in the wild are now **Wild animals** instead of fully domesticated ones. You must breed them over several generations to obtain proper farm animals.

## Wild vs Farm Animals

| Feature | Wild Animal | Farm Animal |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| Drops | 2/3 of normal drops | Full drops |
| Milking (Cow) | ❌ Impossible | ✅ Possible |
| Saddling (Pig) | ❌ Impossible | ✅ Possible |
| Egg Laying (Chicken) | ❌ None | ✅ Normal |
| Shearing (Sheep) | 1 wool, 1/3 chance to anger | Normal |
| Aggression | Fights back when attacked | Passive |

## Domestication Process

1. Find wild animals in the world
2. Breed two wild animals with their breeding item
3. The offspring is **one generation closer** to domesticated
4. Use the offspring for further breeding
5. Repeat until fully domesticated

> [!NOTE]
> The average number of generations needed is **4** by default. Each generation's offspring is more evolved than its parents.

## Breeding Items

| Animal | Breeding Item |
|--------|-------------|
| Cow | Wheat |
| Sheep | Wheat |
| Pig | Wheat, Apples, Melons, Bread |
| Chicken | Seeds |
| Horse | Golden Apple (wild horses do not exist) |
| Rabbit | Golden Carrot (wild rabbits do not exist) |

## Time Scepter (Cheat/Creative Only)

Available only in creative mode or via cheats:

| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Right-click | Makes a baby animal grow OR resets adult breeding cooldown |
| Left-click | Instantly evolves a wild animal to a farm animal |
