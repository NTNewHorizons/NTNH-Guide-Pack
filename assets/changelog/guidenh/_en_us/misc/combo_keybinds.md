# Combo Keybinds
Combination keybinds massively increase the number of reasonable keybinds you can use at once, solving the problem of running out of keys on smaller keyboards.

<FloatingImage src="./assets/combo_keys.png" wrap="square" width="256" align="left">
  <ImageAnnotation>
    An example of some combo keybinds!
  </ImageAnnotation>
</FloatingImage>
<br clear="all"/>

> [!NOTE]
> Combo Keybinds do not work in interfaces. This is a limitation due to the order of handling within GUIs themselves and is unfortunately unavoidable
