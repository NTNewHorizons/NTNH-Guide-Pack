# Better Grave Recovery
Keep dying to infernal mobs and sick of rearranging your inventory?

__Say less__

OpenBlocks graves <ItemImage id="OpenBlocks:grave" /> work like modern gravestones. Retrieve your items by left-clicking or Shift + right-clicking with an empty hand. Backpacks, tools, baubles, and armor are restored in their original configuration.

Right clicking a grave with an empty hand will show a death message associated with how the player died. Other players will not be able to accidentally open your grave

> [!NOTE]
> The original drop-on-death behavior is still available by right-clicking whilst holding a shovel
