Quartz Fiber can be attached to any side of a cable. It separates the attached [cable](./cables.md) from the cable adjacent to the face it is attached to, preventing the two cables from transmitting channels to each other while still allowing power to pass through. It is commonly used to separate different networks or to plan [channel routing](../ae2-mechanics/channels.md#channel-routing), and it can also be used to provide power from one network to an [ad-hoc network](../ae2-mechanics/channels.md#ad-hoc-networks).
