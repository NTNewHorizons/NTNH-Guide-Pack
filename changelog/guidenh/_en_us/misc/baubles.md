# Baubles
The Baubles system provides <Color id="GREEN">__universal__</Color> bauble slots that accept most baubles in the game. The ability to equip torches and blocks on your head is handled through <Color id="GREEN">Custom Cosmetic Armor</Color>, which also works with <Color id="GREEN">Dynamic Lighting</Color>.
